ring-elevation-profile Component Demo

ring-elevation-profile — SVG elevation profile of the Nürburgring Nordschleife (≈300 m of elevation over 20.8 km). Renders an area chart from GPX data (via the gpx-url attribute or ) with optional grid, metric or imperial axis labels, and a hover tooltip showing distance, elevation, and the track section under the pointer.
